About Second Culture

I started Second Culture making yogurt from scratch in my college apartment because I believe yogurt bowls can be the next big thing — following the boba and matcha waves before.

Our yogurt is double-strained for maximum thickness and high protein, highlighting Chinese and multicultural flavors. Each bowl is thoughtfully crafted for a unique balance of textures and flavors, from Ube Crème Brûlée to Black Sesame Kinako.

The name "Second Culture" carries a double meaning: culture as in yogurt — but also my background as a Chinese-American, connecting my two cultures through flavor.
