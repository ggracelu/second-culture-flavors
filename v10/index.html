Our Story

Yogurt bowls are
the next big thing.

I started Second Culture by making yogurt from scratch in my college apartment because I believe that yogurt bowls can be the next big thing following the boba and matcha crazes.

Our yogurt is double strained for maximum thickness and high protein, and highlights Chinese and multicultural flavors. Each bowl is thoughtfully crafted for a unique balance of textures and flavors, from Ube Creme Brulee to Black Sesame Kinako.

Our brand name, Second Culture, is based on the double meaning of the word culture: culture as in yogurt, of course, but also bringing in my background as a Chinese-American to connect my two cultures through flavor.

Questions?
We've got answers.

We host pop-ups at my apartment in Hyde Park, Chicago on a biweekly basis.
We use a pre-order system, accepting orders the Monday before the pop-up takes place on Sunday. Due to limited capacity, we sell out quickly so we recommend signing up through our interest form to get priority access.
Each bowl is $8 and comes with 5.3 oz of our double-strained homemade yogurt and 4+ toppings, with exact ingredients varying by the flavor. The portion size is similar to yogurt bowls at brunch restaurants or acai bowls.
We are happy to remove any toppings upon request. However, the base yogurt recipe cannot be changed to order, and it is made from whole milk.
